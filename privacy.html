Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2025

MinMen Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the DragonFish mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

DragonFish is designed to work entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or location) ourselves.

However, the App uses third-party SDKs that may collect certain data:

  • Google AdMob — serves in-app advertisements. AdMob may collect:
    • Device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID / IDFA)
    • IP address and coarse location (derived from IP)
    • Device type, OS version, and app version
    • Ad interaction events (impressions, clicks)

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.

2. How the Information Is Used

Data collected by Google AdMob is used to:

  • Serve relevant advertisements within the App
  • Measure ad performance and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

All game data (moves, positions, history) is stored locally on your device only via iOS UserDefaults. It is never transmitted to any server.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob — Google's mobile advertising platform.
    Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy to understand how they handle data. You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your device's privacy settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → turn off "Personalised Ads"

4. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14.5 and later, the App will request your permission before accessing your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA) via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You may deny this permission; the App will continue to function fully, and only non-personalised ads will be served.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us at the address below and we will take steps to delete such information.

6. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data on our own servers. Any data collected by Google AdMob is subject to Google's own data retention policies.

7. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect information processed within the App. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to review the security practices of any third-party services integrated into the App.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
